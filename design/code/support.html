Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password by clicking the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. An email with instructions will be sent to your registered email address.

What are the trading fees?

Our trading fees are 0.1% for both makers and takers. Volume-based discounts are available. Please check our fees page for more details.

How long do withdrawals take?

Crypto withdrawals are typically processed within 30 minutes, but can take up to 24 hours depending on network congestion.
